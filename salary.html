Salary & Job Forecast

Understanding the financial outlook and future demand for plumbers

Salary Expectations

Plumbers earn competitive salaries that increase with experience, specialization and skill. According to recent data:

  • Apprentice Plumbers: Around $18 - $22 an hour
  • Entry-Level Plumbers: Around $36,000 - $50,000 per year
  • Mid-Level Plumbers: Around $50,000 - $80,000 per year
  • Experienced/Master Plumbers: Over $90,000 per year

Job Growth & Demand

The demand for plumbers is expected to remain strong due to aging infrastructure and the constant need for plumbing maintenance and service.

  • The Bureau of Labor Statistics (BLS) projects a 2% job growth from 2022 to 2032, keeping pace with overall job market trends.
  • High retirement rates among experienced plumbers are creating new job openings.
  • Green plumbing technologies and water efficiency initiatives are increasing demand for specialized plumbers.

Industry Trends

Plumbing continues to evolve with new technology and sustainability efforts. Trends of the future include:

  • Increased use of smart plumbing systems for leak detection and efficiency.
  • Growing demand for eco-friendly plumbing solutions to conserve water and energy.
  • Expansion of apprenticeship programs to address workforce shortages.
