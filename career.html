Investment Broker Career Overview

What is an Investment Broker?

An investment broker is a professional who buys and sells stocks, bonds, and mutual funds on behalf of clients. They assist individual investors and large institutions by advising on investments to meet specific financial goals. Brokers can either work under a firm or operate independently.

Industry Insights

Investment brokers can work for firms like Goldman Sachs, Morgan Stanley, and Charles Schwab.
